# How to Ask CerSafe AI Chatbot

CerSafe AI is specialized in providing information about college events. For the best results, use clear and specific questions. Here are some examples:

## 📅 Event Discovery
- "What are the upcoming events this month?"
- "Are there any hackathons scheduled soon?"
- "Can you show me a list of all technical events?"
- "What sports events can I participate in?"

## ℹ️ Event Details
- "Tell me more about the [Event Name]."
- "What is the venue for [Event Name]?"
- "What time does the [Event Name] start?"
- "Who can I contact for information about [Event Name]?"

## 📝 Registration & Fees
- "What is the registration fee for [Event Name]?"
- "How do I register for the upcoming hackathon?"
- "Are there any free events available?"
- "What is the team size for [Event Name]?"

## 🏆 Rules & Prizes
- "What are the rules for the [Event Name]?"
- "What prizes are being offered for the first place in [Event Name]?"
- "Is there any specific software required for [Event Name]?"

---

> [!TIP]
> If the chatbot can't find specific details, it might be because the event hasn't been added to the portal yet. Try asking for a general list of events first!
